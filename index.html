01 · About Cyber Safety

The internet is amazing. But not without rules.

// Daily reality

Every device. Every login. Every click counts.

Cyber security is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and personal data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. In daily life — whether we are banking, studying, shopping or socializing — we share information online constantly.

Protecting your personal information matters because a single leaked password, a careless OTP share, or one clicked phishing link can lead to identity theft, financial loss, or even harassment. Awareness is your strongest firewall.
